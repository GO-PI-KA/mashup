Ind vs Eng, 5th Test: The curious case of KL Rahul's quadric

Senior India batsman KL Rahul on Monday was ruled out of the third Test against England due to his incomplete recovery from a quadriceps strain, which had sidelined him for the second match in Visakhapatnam.

In his place, Devdutt Padikkal, the promising left-handed batsman from Karnataka, renowned for his stellar performance in the first-class season, has been included in the squad.
